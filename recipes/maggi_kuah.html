Maggi Kuah

Ingredient

  • 1 packet of Maggi Kari(other flavor also can)
  • water
  • fish cake/fish ball (quantities your choice)
  • small chopped carrot
  • chicken egg

Instruction

  1. Heat water using pot until water boil
  2. put small chopped carrot fish cake and fish ball in boiling water
  3. Put Maggi Kari with flavour
  4. Seasoning (taste depends on your)
  5. Put egg to put
  6. After 5 minutes turn off kitchen and dish ready to eat
